Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a slot?

Register, login,select location, click on book slot,choose a venue, select date and time, complete payment and confirm your booking.

Do I need an account to book a slot?

Yes. You must register and login before booking any sports venue.

Can I book a slot for a past date?

No. Only today's date and future dates are available.

What payment methods are available?

UPI, Card and Cash payment options are supported.

What happens after successful payment?

Your booking confirmation page will display venue, date, time slot and payment details.

Why can't I move to the next booking step?

Make sure venue, date, time slot and payment methods are selected.
